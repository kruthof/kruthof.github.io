DriftBench — Human Validation Study

Context: We are evaluating whether AI models preserve the original research constraints when iteratively refining research proposals. Each item shows a research question with specific hard constraints (e.g., budget limits, sample sizes, methodological requirements), and a proposal generated by an AI model.

Your task: For each hard constraint, judge whether the AI's proposal respects it:
  • Satisfied — the proposal clearly and explicitly respects this constraint
  • Stretched — technically respected but pushed to its boundaries or handled ambiguously
  • Violated — the proposal clearly ignores or contradicts this constraint
Important:
  • Read the full proposal before scoring — violations are sometimes subtle
  • Judge the proposal content, not whether the idea is good or creative
  • Some proposals have no violations; some have several — there is no expected distribution
  • You do not need domain expertise — constraints are designed to be objectively checkable
Time: ~2 hours for all 55 items. Your progress is saved in your browser — you can close and come back anytime.

When done: Click "Download Scores" and send the JSON file to the study coordinator.
